About B-Hub

B-Hub is a reimagined BBA program that redefines business education for the modern world. Affiliated with Andhra University, it seamlessly integrates a rigorous BBA curriculum with comprehensive CAT coaching and hands-on profile building, creating a unified pathway to top-tier B-schools and future leadership roles.

Inspired by the academic rigor and mentorship culture of premier institutions like the IIMs, B-Hub offers more than just a degree—it delivers a transformative journey.
